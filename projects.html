Errandz

Errandz is a mobile platform application which provides solution for people who are looking for odd jobs in order to earn quick money. For example, the high school kids who are too young to be working full time but are old enough to help someone in the neighbourhood with everyday chores like babysitting, lawn mowing etc. will find this application extremely useful.

It consist of many features like the user will be able to look for work opportunities (odd jobs) within 10km radius of his location. This applies to both the types of users: the one looking for a job and the one who wants to get some work done. Hirer and worker will be able to review and rate each other for future opportunities and recommendations etc.

Grab Grub

Grab Grub is a mobile platform focused on food courts. With this application, we are trying to help users avoid long waiting queues at food courts to place their orders. They can use this application to place their orders from any outlet inside a particular food court and will be notified when their order is ready. They will just need to pick it up from the mentioned counter when they get a notification on their phones. Our vision is to save the user from the frustration of standing in a long queue and waiting.

User will see food courts nearby to their location and can also see the information related that food court. They can order from multiple outlet within same food court with feature of seeing at what time their order will be ready.
