About Me

Being born in the family with a business in IT, as a child I was always around laptops and computers and always had a keen interest in figuring out how they work. Many people consider exposing your child to video games as a bad thing but my father had a reason to do so. He realized the thrust that I was having for technology and he was right. At a very early age I began editing games starting by changing colors, textures, and then bodies of cars and boo yeah I was thrown into the world of programming.

I knew to further take my passion I need to have a degree in this domain so I graduated with a Bachelor of Technologies (Computer Science) degree. During my time in college, I was having it hard to decide which framework to choose to create games so I literally created one game in 4 different frameworks (Construct 2, Unity Game Engine, Corona SDK, and LibGDX) and finally released the game via libGDX.

During my graduation, I also worked as an Intern Android Developer for summertime where I was given the task to reverse engineer, upgrade and release a game that was built in Cocos2dx (Ha-ha! another framework). The same company took me in after my graduation where I created and released many mobile applications. I suggested and improved the work environment of the company by using MVP Architecture, Material Designs, etc. All of the animation work was assigned to me as I had an inherent trait for that.
